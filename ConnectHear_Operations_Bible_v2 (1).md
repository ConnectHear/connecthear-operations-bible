### Workstream: Tax Compliance

**Description:** File all required taxes. ConnectHear uses **cash basis accounting** - revenue only reported when payment received.

**Tax Schedule:**
- **Monthly:** (1) Payroll Tax on payday, (2) Sales Tax 15% on payments received that month, (3) Withholding Tax 4% on vendor payments
- **Quarterly:** Advance Income Tax (AIT) - prediction and prepayment
- **Annual:** Income Tax - final filing reconciling AIT with actual profit

**Frequency:** Daily/Monthly/Quarterly/Annual

**Output:** Tax returns filed on time, payment receipts, calculation documentation, compliance records

**RACI:**

| Role | R | A | C | I |
|------|---|---|---|---|
| Finance Associate (Umaima) | ‚úÖ | | | |

**Critical Understanding - Cash Basis:**
- **Example:** UBL hasn't paid 2023/2024 invoices
- ConnectHear has NOT reported this revenue to FBR (payment not received)
- When UBL pays in 2025, revenue reported in 2025 tax filings
- **Benefit:** Don't pay tax on money not yet received
- **Implication:** Accounts receivable tracking is critical
