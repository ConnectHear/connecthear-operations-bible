Lead Sourcing & Tracking
Frequency
Weekly (CRM updates), Daily (new leads)
Owner
BD Associate (Hiba/Ahmed)

Description

Maintaining CRM database with all incoming leads from multiple sources including direct inquiries, indirect sources (interpreters receiving requests, event interactions, leadership networking), social media, and referrals. Track lead progression through defined pipeline stages.

Output

  • Updated CRM sheet with all leads
  • Lead status tracking with pipeline stages: New ‚Üí Contacted ‚Üí Qualified ‚Üí Won/Lost
  • Indirect lead register with source attribution

Dependencies

  • Marketing (for social media traffic and campaigns)
  • Sign Language Department (for interpreter-sourced leads)
  • Leadership (for networking-sourced opportunities)

Key Process Notes

  • Indirect Lead Scenarios:
    • Interpreters receive calls from employees (4 free calls, then 5th triggers BD contact)
    • Interpreters collect company requests at events and forward to BD
    • Leadership meets potential clients at events/conferences
  • Tracking Requirements: Simple stage tracking for incentive calculation
  • Lead Qualification: Record employer details, decision-maker info, and service interest
