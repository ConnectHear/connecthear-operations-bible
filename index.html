Contracting & Closure

Frequency: Per Deal
Owner: BD Associate, BD Manager/Azima
Quick Jump:
  • üìù Description
  • üìä Output
  • üîó Dependencies
  • üë• RACI Matrix
  • üí° Notes

Description

Finalize contracts with legal and financial terms, obtain signatures from all parties, and archive signed agreements. Critical Gap: Currently only app services have proper contracts (Digital Services Agreement). Training and interpretation services rely on MOUs which are not legally binding and create revenue recognition and payment risks.

Output

  • Signed contract (Digital Services Agreement for app, Service Agreement for training/interpretation)
  • Mandatory: Purchase Order (PO) from client before service delivery begins
  • Contract archived in shared drive with proper naming convention
  • Contract terms logged in CRM for renewal tracking

Dependencies

  • Finance ‚Äî for contract review, terms approval, and PO verification
  • Legal ‚Äî for contract language and compliance, if applicable
  • Leadership ‚Äî for final approval on significant deals

RACI Matrix

Role R A C I
BD Associate ‚úÖ
BD Manager/Azima ‚úÖ
Finance Associate ‚úÖ
Leadership ‚úÖ
Critical Implementation Requirements
  • New Policy: No service delivery without Purchase Order (PO) from client
  • Contract Development Needed: Create standard Service Agreements for:
  • Interpretation services (hourly/event-based)
  • Training programs (corporate/public batches)
  • Revenue Protection: Contracts ensure proper invoicing, payment terms, and reduce delayed payment risk
  • PO Requirement: Ensures client's finance department is aware and payment is budgeted
